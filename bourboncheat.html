The Styles
Standard BourbonStart Here▼
ProfileSweet, approachable, balanced
NotesCaramel, vanilla, toasted oak, baking spice
ExamplesBuffalo Trace, Wild Turkey 101, Four Roses
Pairs withBBQ ribs, pecan pie, aged cheddar
Best forThe classic introduction — the flavor profile most people picture when they think "bourbon"
Wheated Bourbon▼
ProfileSofter, rounder, creamier — rye swapped for wheat
NotesHoney, soft wheat bread, red fruit, gentle spice
ExamplesMaker's Mark, W.L. Weller, Larceny
Pairs withRoast pork, glazed donuts, vanilla desserts
Best forDrinkers who find rye-forward bourbons too spicy or peppery
High-Rye BourbonBold▼
ProfileSpicy, bold, vibrant — more rye in the mash bill
NotesBlack pepper, cinnamon, clove, rye bread
ExamplesBulleit Bourbon, Knob Creek, Four Roses Single Barrel
Pairs withSpicy food, charcuterie, dark chocolate
Best forFans of bolder, drier flavors — also the best bridge into rye whiskey
Bottled-in-BondSpecialty▼
ProfileRobust, consistent, always 100 proof by law
NotesRich oak, dark chocolate, maple syrup, dried fruit
ExamplesOld Grand-Dad BiB, Henry McKenna 10 Year, Evan Williams BiB
Pairs withSmoked brisket, dark chocolate, blue cheese
Heads up"Bottled-in-Bond" is a federal designation, not a brand — it's one of the best value-for-quality signals on a shelf.
Cocktails
Old Fashioned▼
MixBourbon, sugar, Angostura bitters, orange twist
StyleTimeless · Spirit-forward · Balanced
Order tipThe benchmark bourbon cocktail — if a bar makes this well, they probably make everything well.
Mint Julep▼
MixBourbon, fresh mint, simple syrup, crushed ice
StyleRefreshing · Aromatic · Cool
Order tipTraditionally served in a frosted silver cup — the Kentucky Derby's signature drink. Best on a hot day.
Whiskey Sour▼
MixBourbon, lemon juice, simple syrup, egg white (optional)
StyleTangy · Silky · Bright
Order tipAsk for it "with egg white" for the classic frothy texture and smoother mouthfeel.
Boulevardier▼
MixBourbon, Campari, sweet vermouth
StyleSophisticated · Bitter · Complex
Order tipEssentially a Negroni with bourbon instead of gin — a great order if you like Negronis but want something a touch sweeter and warmer.
Kentucky Mule▼
MixBourbon, ginger beer, lime juice
StyleSpicy · Refreshing · Tangy
Order tipA Moscow Mule with bourbon — easy-drinking and a safe choice if you're not sure what else to order.
